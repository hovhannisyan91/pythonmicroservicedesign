Project Overview

Objective

Streamline and optimize the outbound call process at the telecom company (any service providing company).

Scope

  • Customer Analytics
    • integrate machine learning models and rule-based segmentation for targeting
  • Data and Insights
    • implement centralized campaign management, call outcomes recording, and post-analysis
  • Automation
    • eliminate manual file sharing to prevent information loss between departments.

Previous State

  • Outbound call process relied on manual workflows
  • Fragmented data exchanges between call center, marketing, and CRM systems,
  • Inefficiencies due to file-based sharing prone to errors and information loss.
  • Limited or outdated data leading to suboptimal targeting and customer engagement

Previous State

Lack of synchronization between departments and no unified platform for managing campaigns, call outcomes, and analysis.

Initiative

Solution

Team
  • Our Team:
    • Data Engineer/Architect/Back-End Engineer
    • 2 Data Analysts/Front-End
    • 3 Data Scientists/Back-End Engineers
    • DevOps Engineer
  • Challenges:
    • Overcame data integration challenges
    • Overcame development challenges
    • Streamlined interdepartmental collaboration to reduce communication gaps

Results and Impact

  • Increased Efficiency
    • Eliminated information loss through automation and integration.
    • Reduced operational delays and errors in outbound calls.
  • Improved Targeting
    • Enhanced customer engagement by integrating data-driven models.
  • Better Post-Call Analysis
    • Provided actionable insights that informed future strategies.

Data-Driven Culture

  • Culture Shift
    • Fostered a culture of data-driven decision-making across teams
    • Introduced dashboards and scorecards as standard tools for campaign adjustments
  • Business Outcomes
    • Data-led strategies increased customer engagement rates.
    • Real-time adjustments improved campaign performance and resource allocation.
